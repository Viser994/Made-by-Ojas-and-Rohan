# MS St. Louis Memorial Website - Design Brainstorm

## Design Approach 1: Solemn Minimalism with Archival Aesthetic
**Design Movement:** Archival Modernism / Historical Documentation  
**Core Principles:**
- Restrained color palette dominated by greys, blacks, and cream whites
- Typography-first approach with generous whitespace
- Sparse, intentional imagery—only historically significant photographs
- Quiet, contemplative interaction patterns

**Color Philosophy:** Neutral tones evoke the weight of historical documentation and the gravity of loss. Cream whites suggest aged paper and archival materials. Deep charcoals provide contrast without aggression.

**Layout Paradigm:** Vertical timeline-based structure with left-aligned text and right-aligned imagery, creating an asymmetric flow that guides readers through the narrative chronologically.

**Signature Elements:**
- Faded historical photographs with subtle grain overlay
- Thin, elegant dividing lines between sections
- Handwritten-style serif font for quotes and memorial text

**Interaction Philosophy:** Minimal, respectful interactions. Smooth scrolling reveals content gradually. No flashy animations—only subtle fade-ins and gentle transitions.

**Animation:** Slow fade-in on scroll (2-3 second duration), gentle blur-to-focus effects on images, subtle underline animations on hover for links.

**Typography System:** Serif body (Georgia or Crimson Text) paired with a clean sans-serif header (Lato or Montserrat). High contrast between weights for hierarchy.

**Probability:** 0.08

## Design Approach 2: Symbolic Geometric Abstraction
**Design Movement:** Contemporary Memorialization / Abstract Symbolism  
**Core Principles:**
- Geometric shapes represent the 937 passengers (dots, lines, or small squares)
- Bold accent color (deep blue or gold) against neutral backgrounds
- Data visualization elements that tell the story through visual metaphor
- Modern, forward-looking design that honors without being oppressive

**Color Philosophy:** A single bold accent color (e.g., deep sapphire blue or burnished gold) represents hope and remembrance. Paired with soft greys and whites to avoid visual heaviness.

**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric grid with geometric sections. Use diagonal cuts and layered panels to create visual interest while maintaining sophistication.

**Signature Elements:**
- 937 small geometric shapes (dots or squares) representing each passenger
- Animated counter that tallies the number of passengers
- Bold accent color used strategically for emphasis and navigation

**Interaction Philosophy:** Interactive elements that invite exploration. Hover over passenger representations to reveal individual stories. Animations that feel purposeful and meaningful.

**Animation:** Staggered entrance animations for geometric elements (dots appearing one by one), smooth transitions between sections, hover effects that reveal hidden information.

**Typography System:** Modern sans-serif (Poppins or Raleway) for headers with a clean, readable sans-serif (Open Sans) for body text. Minimal serif accents for quotes.

**Probability:** 0.07

## Design Approach 3: Emotional Narrative with Textured Depth
**Design Movement:** Contemporary Storytelling / Emotional Realism  
**Core Principles:**
- Layered, textured backgrounds that evoke depth and emotion
- Large, impactful imagery that draws viewers into the human story
- Warm, muted earth tones with occasional bold accent colors
- Narrative-driven structure that prioritizes human stories over facts

**Color Philosophy:** Warm greys, muted ochres, and soft blues create an intimate, reflective atmosphere. Occasional warm accent colors (rust, terracotta) provide emotional punctuation.

**Layout Paradigm:** Organic, flowing layout with overlapping sections. Images and text blend together, creating a collage-like effect that feels handcrafted and intentional.

**Signature Elements:**
- Large hero image of the memorial stone (generated image)
- Textured background overlays (paper grain, subtle patterns)
- Hand-drawn or organic dividers between sections
- Embedded quotes with personal stories

**Interaction Philosophy:** Immersive, emotionally resonant interactions. Parallax scrolling, image reveals, and story-driven animations that deepen emotional connection.

**Animation:** Parallax scrolling effects, image zoom on scroll, text fade-in with staggered timing, subtle background animations (moving textures).

**Typography System:** Serif header font (Playfair Display or EB Garamond) for emotional impact, paired with a warm, readable sans-serif (Lato or Nunito) for body text.

**Probability:** 0.06

---

## Selected Design Approach: **Emotional Narrative with Textured Depth**

This approach was chosen because it best serves the memorial's purpose: **to honor the human tragedy and inform people about this historical event.** The emotional, narrative-driven design creates a space for reflection and connection, making the story of the 937 passengers deeply personal and memorable.

The warm, textured aesthetic feels respectful without being oppressive, and the large, impactful imagery will draw visitors into the human story. This design philosophy will guide all decisions moving forward.
